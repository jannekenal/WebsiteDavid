Welcome to my Resurrection Book site!

I am David Vanriper, and have published my first book.

THE RESURRECTION OF CHRIST: Reconciling the gospel accounts

Primarily though, my desire is that you check out my work on the resurrection of Christ. If you, like me, have always been confused by the many differences in the gospel accounts of the events of Easter morning,and the days following; this is the book you need to read! This work places every verse - in certain instances, even individual clauses - in proper chronological order, resulting in a consistent, flowing narrative. The order of events as I record them are based upon deep study, common sense, and prayer.and above all, faith in the veracity of scripture. You will discover that.

  • There were likely three groups of women who visited the tomb Sunday morning, mere minutes apart.
  • Christ briefly ascended to heaven, and returned after His first appearance to Mary Magdalene.
  • Peter visited the empty sepulcher twice that Sunday morning. I hope I have piqued your interest! Thank you for visiting, and come back soon! Oh, and feel free to leave comments and feedback!
